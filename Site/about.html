ButterDogCo's Story

You may be asking yourself, what's ButterDogCo? ButterDogCo is a 100% legal company that is owned by Keegan, and co-owned by Kai (The COO).

We were created in 2021 inside of a middle school science class. When we were created, the entire company was based around a "really good" search engine called ButterDog Search.

Later, ButterDogCo began expanding into multiple different websites, later called "apps." This is where we are now, no longer on Google Sites, and with a relatively large and friendly community.
